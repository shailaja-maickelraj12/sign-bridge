How to use
Step 01
📷
Allow Camera

Click "Start Camera" and allow browser camera access. SignBridge uses your camera to see your hand gestures in real time.

Step 02
🤚
Show a Gesture

Hold your hand clearly in front of the camera. Keep the gesture steady for about 1 second until the AI detects it.

Step 03
🔊
Hear the Speech

Your gesture is translated to English + Tamil (or chosen language) and spoken aloud automatically with text displayed.

Mode A
🎙️
Interview Mode

Full-screen display for in-person interviews. The interviewer sees your signed responses on a clean, distraction-free screen.

Mode B
🌐
Online Interview + Training

Share your screen on Zoom / Meet / Teams. Use Scenario Training to practice 15-step conversation scripts before your interview.
